:::
title: "LLM Benchmark 2025"
subtitle: "Insights for Executives – April Edition"
:::

:::
title: "Key Highlights"
items:
  - New OpenAI models (o3-mini, o4-mini, GPT-4.1) dominate reasoning benchmarks.
  - Qwen3 models redefine on-premises AI performance.
  - Google Gemini 2.5 series gains traction in enterprise scenarios.
  - Practical learnings from the “Embrace AI” initiative show productivity boosts through prompt mastery.
:::

:::
title: "OpenAI Models – Strong but Costly"
items:
  - o3-mini and o4-mini rank at the top for reasoning quality.
  - GPT-4.1 variants (base and mini) remain strong but not top tier.
  - Cost of usage remains a key drawback compared to competitors.
:::

:::
title: "Qwen3 – State-of-the-Art for Local AI"
items:
  - Major leap from Qwen 2.5 to Qwen3, with 32.8B parameter flagship models.
  - MoE architecture (Qwen3-30B-A3B) activates only 3.3B parameters per token, improving efficiency.
  - Enterprise-grade accuracy suitable for overnight or batch workloads.
:::

:::
title: "Google Gemini 2.5 – The Rising Contender"
items:
  - Gemini 2.5 Pro and Flash models enter top-10 leaderboard.
  - Outperform OpenAI on price–stability ratio.
  - Excels in large-context tasks (200k–500k tokens) and document understanding.
  - Best-in-class for complex, multi-page compliance document processing.
:::

:::
title: "Market Implications"
items:
  - Google rapidly closing the gap with OpenAI.
  - Qwen3 threatens cloud dominance with on-prem performance.
  - Model selection increasingly depends on workflow type and context size.
:::

:::
title: "AI Coding Insights – Embrace AI Initiative"
items:
  - Senior engineers shifting from OpenAI and Claude 3.7 to Gemini 2.5 Pro.
  - Decision drivers: context size, quality, speed, and cost.
  - Practical kata shows that skilled prompt engineering beats multi-agent IDEs.
:::

:::
title: "Prompt Distillation Case Study"
items:
  - Task: build a simple WebUI LLM app using prompt-only methods.
  - Best results: 15-minute completions using GPT-o1 Pro and Gemini 2.5 Pro.
  - 70-token distilled prompts proved effective across multiple models.
  - Demonstrates the power of concise, model-agnostic prompt design.
:::

:::
title: "Key Takeaways for Leaders"
items:
  - AI productivity depends more on practitioner skill than tooling complexity.
  - Claude 3.7 Sonnet and Gemini 2.5 Pro are current favorites for AI Coding.
  - Strategic investment should balance model capability, cost, and workflow fit.
  - Continuous skill development (“prompt literacy”) drives sustainable advantage.
:::

:::
title: "From Benchmark to Business Value"
items:
  - Move from experimentation to enterprise-ready deployment.
  - Focus on context-rich workflows, document intelligence, and human-AI synergy.
  - Turning AI potential into measurable business impact.
:::
