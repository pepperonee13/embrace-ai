Logo Component

The TIMETOACT GROUP logo MUST appear on every slide. Use the provided size classes and positioning utilities to place the logo correctly. Never distort, rotate, or alter the logo colors.

Logo Positioning

Use positioning utilities for absolute placement on slides. Combine size and position classes.

Available position classes:

  • tta-logo-position-top-left - Top left corner (40px, 80px)
  • tta-logo-position-top-right - Top right corner (recommended)
  • tta-logo-position-bottom-left - Bottom left corner
  • tta-logo-position-bottom-right - Bottom right corner

Usage Guidelines

Accessibility & Contrast

  • Always maintain WCAG AA contrast standards (4.5:1 for normal text, 3:1 for large text)
  • Use dark colors (Dark Gunmetal, Dark Petrol, Dark Blue) on light backgrounds
  • Use light colors on dark backgrounds
  • Test color combinations for readability before deploying

Best Practices

  • Include the TIMETOACT logo on every slide
  • Use Orange sparingly for maximum impact on highlights and CTAs
  • Maintain generous whitespace for clean, professional layouts
  • Limit bullet points to 5-6 per slide for readability
  • Use gradients for hero sections and emphasis areas
  • Keep slides focused on a single main idea
